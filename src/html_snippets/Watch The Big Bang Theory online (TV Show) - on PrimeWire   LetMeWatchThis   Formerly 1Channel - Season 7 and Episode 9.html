Leonard Hofstadter and Sheldon Cooper are both brilliant physicists working at Cal Tech in Pasadena, California. They are colleagues, and roommates, although in all capacities their relationship is always tested primarily by Sheldon's regimented and non-conventional ways. They are also friends with their Cal Tech colleagues, mechanical engineer Howard Wolowitz and astrophysicist Rajesh Koothrappali. The foursome spend their time working on their individual work projects, playing video games, watching science fiction movies or reading comic books. As they are self-professed nerds, all have little or no luck with "popular" women. When Penny, a pretty woman and an aspiring actress originally from Omaha, moves into the apartment next to Leonard and Sheldon's, Leonard has another aspiration in life, namely to get Penny to be his girlfriend.

4 / 5 - Posted by LordGiggles 4 months ago.
100% Useful

This, for me, is one of those great shows where you can just decide "I feel like watching a big bang theory episode" then pick one at random, watch it and laugh.

Whilst there is a story line to it that progresses throughout the seasons it is not crucial for you to know the ins and outs of the show, which makes it an ideal time filler.

The 20 or so minute length of the episodes work in its favour, in my opinion, as it keeps them short, sweet and leaving you wanting more. If the episodes were longer it'd be easy to lose interest as it is not the type of show that will keep you engaged for long periods of time due to a lack of in-depth storyline.

As far as sitcoms go this is one of my favourites for the pure reason that it isn't filled with crude humour or the overly-used exploits of a single ladies-man. Instead it relies on its array of unique characters, intelligent humour, wit and the occasional sarcastic comment to get a genuine laugh.
